# People to Follow

Smart money. Signal-to-noise matters more than fame.

## Institutional (13F via WhaleWisdom / HedgeFollow — free tiers)
- **Stanley Druckenmiller (Duquesne)** — macro, concentrated bets. Low S/N but every move is a thesis.
- **David Tepper (Appaloosa)** — value + macro overlay. Follow the big adds.
- **Michael Burry (Scion)** — contrarian. Signal usually 6–18 months early.
- **Bill Ackman (Pershing Square)** — long-duration, public on his thesis.
- **Renaissance Technologies** — quant, impossible to follow but changes signal broad factor tilt.

Where: whalewisdom.com, hedgefollow.com. 13Fs lag 45 days — use for direction, not timing.

## Congressional / insider
- **CapitolTrades.com** — free, near-real-time congressional disclosures.
- **Quiver Quant Congress Trading** — free leaderboard of top-performing lawmakers.
- Specifically watch: Nancy Pelosi (frequent tech large positions), Dan Crenshaw, Tommy Tuberville.
- **OpenInsider.com** — free insider transactions. Cluster buys by execs are the signal.

## Options flow (free adjacents since we can't afford Unusual Whales yet)
- **X / StockTwits** accounts that post sweeps: search for "unusual options" aggregators.
- **Barchart** free section has top options volume and unusual activity tables.
- **Cheddar Flow** / **FlowAlgo** — paid; not yet.

## Macro / narrative
- **Zerohedge** — cynical. Read for what narratives are pricing in.
- **The Daily Shot** (WSJ) — chart aggregator, disciplined.
- **FedWatch (CME)** — free rate expectations.

## Retail sentiment (contrarian indicator)
- **r/wallstreetbets daily thread** — for extreme positioning, not for ideas.
- **StockTwits trending** — for momentum froth detection.
