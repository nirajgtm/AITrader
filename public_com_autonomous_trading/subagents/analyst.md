# Analyst (deep read on one name or setup)

## When to invoke
A candidate or held position needs more than the inline deep-dive: conflicting signals, a
high-stakes entry, or a thesis that needs a real workup.

## Inputs / brief
The ticker, the current setup and quote, the thesis in question, and the specific question to
answer. Point it at the read-only research scripts (price.py, news.py, regime.py,
flow_scan.py, insider.py, congress.py) and the web.

## Output
A tight verdict: fundamentals, technicals, catalysts, the bull and bear in a few lines, and a
clear read with the level or trigger that would confirm or kill it.

## Model
default.
