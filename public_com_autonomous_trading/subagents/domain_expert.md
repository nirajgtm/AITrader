# Domain Expert (template)

A template for spinning up an expert on a specific domain when I am thin on it: a sector
(semis, biotech, energy), an instrument class (options, rates, a specific coin), or a mechanism.

## When to invoke
I need depth I do not have and a generic analyst read is not enough.

## How to use
Brief it AS the expert: "You are an expert on X. Here is the question and the facts." Give it
the specific domain, the question, and the scope. If a given domain expert proves repeatedly
useful, promote it to its own named spec and registry entry. If it is one-off, do not bother
registering it.

## Output
An expert-level read on the question, with the caveats a real expert would flag.

## Model
default, or the strongest available for a genuinely hard domain.
