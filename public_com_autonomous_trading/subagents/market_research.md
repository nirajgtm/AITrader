# Market Research (sector, regime, flows, structure)

## When to invoke
Updating the worldview, or sizing a macro, regime, or sector question that a single name read
cannot answer.

## Inputs / brief
The question (example: is the AI-cooling trade crowded; is the regime risk-on or risk-off),
the relevant tickers or sectors, and the time horizon. Use regime.py, flow_scan.py, and the web.

## Output
A structural read: what is changing, the evidence, what it implies for our setup, and the
confidence. Feeds the worldview, not a single trade.

## Model
default.
