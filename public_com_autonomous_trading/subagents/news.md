# News (catalysts and why something is moving)

## When to invoke
A name or the broader tape is moving and I need to know WHY before acting, or I am building
the macro picture for the day.

## Inputs / brief
The ticker(s) or theme, the time window, and what decision the news feeds. Use news.py and the
web; prefer primary sources and dated items.

## Output
The catalyst(s) with dates, whether they look priced in, and the read: a real driver, noise,
or a falling knife. Flag anything stale or low-confidence.

## Model
default.
